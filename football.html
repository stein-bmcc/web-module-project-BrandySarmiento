All about football

Photo taken by Pixabay

In Football The goal is to score more points than the other team. The offense tries to move the ball down the field and score points in the opposing team's end zone. The defense tries to stop the offense from scoring and regain possession of the ball. When scoring, A touchdown is worth six points, and a field goal is worth three points. After a touchdown, a team can kick for an extra point or pass or run for two extra points. when tackling in this sport Legal tackles are below the neck and above the knees. Tackling with the head first, also known as "spearing," is illegal and dangerous.

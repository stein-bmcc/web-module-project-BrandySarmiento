All about basketball

Photo taken by Wallace Chuck

Basketball is a sport where you need to score hoops with a basketball, meanwhile an opponent is going to try to score hoops as well and each opponent need to prevent eachother from scoring hoops by taking the ball alway from them or blocking their shots. In this game points are counted by the distance you shoot the ball from so if you shoot the ball withing the big line then that would be a 1 point shot, if they shoot from the line or behind the 3 point line and make the shot then they get 3 points. People play this sport competitively too in a thing called the NBA and to this day have matches between plenty of teams that all fight to be the #1 team in the world of basketball. There are also these things called "Foul Play" that comes into lay when someone from an oppposing team injures or plays too rough causing the other person to either get hurt or worse, these foul plays are put in place to punish those who play rough and gives the person who got injured by a foul play a free shot/foul shot which they can take from the one point line.
